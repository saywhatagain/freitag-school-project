Inside FREITAG

FREITAG bags are made by real people and only a few are called to this vocation. Our crew in the F-actory works extremely hard and produces one-of-a-kind bags. They have jobs that don’t exist anywhere else in the world and which you can only learn here. No one knows more about truck-tarp work-of-art manufacturing than our guys and gals in the Cutting Department, Laundry Department and Bag Design. So, we’ve decided to let them explain how everything functions at the F-actory
