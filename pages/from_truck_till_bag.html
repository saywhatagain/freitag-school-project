Individual Recycled Freewaybags

Since 1993, FREITAG has been creating bags & accessories from used materials that were once at home on the streets and thus contribute all of their own stories and unique features. Just as the FREITAG raw materials – used truck tarps, bicycle inner tubes, seatbelts – are marked by their first stage of life on the streets of Europe, the esthetics and finish of every FREITAG bag produce an unmistakably unique creation.

How does it happen?

The F-Crew doesn’t work in underground mines, high up on a mountain or on isolated drilling platforms because the raw materials for FREITAG products are on the go on the streets of Europe each and every day – just like our Truck Hunters, who hunt for the truck tarps with the boldest designs, the most cheerful colors and truly perfect finishes.

The key to the originality of FREITAG products is the transformation of the tarps from a transport truck cover to a bag exterior. Just to be precise and complicated, we call this process “re-contextualization”. We look at something (a tarp) in one context (on a truck), then we take it and use it in another context (for a bag).
